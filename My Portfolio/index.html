I am Deepali Patel

And I'm a Photographer

ABOUT

I'm a nature photographer. I take picture of people as well as nature.

Born in Gujarat, grew up in Orissa. My first years were spent behind the iron curtain. Now I live in Bangalore. I love to travel random places.

My goal is to show the beauty and grace of nature among people. I focus on non-models and place regular people in exaggerated situations that highlight an aspect of who they are. I look for a sense of defiance and fortitude in my work.

I have a hunger for learning new things.
